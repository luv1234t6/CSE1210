The Cleveland Cavaliers


About

Founded: 1970, the Cleveland Cavaliers are a professional basketball team based in Cleveland, Ohio. They currently play their home games at the Rocket Mortgage FieldHouse, located in downtown Cleveland.

Championships and Success

The Cavaliers have won 1 NBA championship, which they won in 2016 when they defeated the Golden State Warriors in a historic seven-game series. The victory was significant as the Cavs became the first team in NBA history to come back from a 3-1 deficit in the Finals.

Cavaliers Legends

  • LeBron James: Arguably the greatest player in franchise history, LeBron James led the Cavaliers to their only NBA championship in 2016. He was a four-time NBA MVP and played two stints with the team (2003-2010, 2014-2018).
  • Kyrie Irving: A key member of the Cavaliers' 2016 championship win, Irving is known for his incredible ball-handling skills and clutch performances, including his game-winning three-pointer in Game 7 of the 2016 Finals.
  • Brad Daugherty: A standout player in the 1980s, Daugherty was a multiple-time NBA All-Star and a key figure in the Cavaliers' success during that era.
