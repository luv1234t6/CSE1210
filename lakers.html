The Los Angeles Lakers


About

Founded: 1947, the Los Angeles Lakers are one of the most successful and storied franchises in NBA history. They originally played in Minneapolis and were known as the Minneapolis Lakers before moving to Los Angeles in 1960. They currently play their home games at the Crypto.com Arena in Los Angeles, California.

Championships and Success

The Lakers have won 17 NBA championships, one less than the Boston Celtics. Their titles span across different eras, with some of their most notable championships coming in the 1980s, early 2000s, and most recently in 2020 when they defeated the Miami Heat in the Finals.

Lakers Legends

  • Magic Johnson: A five-time NBA champion, Magic Johnson is regarded as one of the greatest point guards of all time. He led the Lakers to five championships during the 1980s, known for his showtime fast breaks and unique passing ability.
  • Kareem Abdul-Jabbar: With six NBA championships and six MVP awards, Abdul-Jabbar is the all-time leading scorer in NBA history. He was a dominant force for the Lakers in the 1980s alongside Magic Johnson.
  • Kobe Bryant: One of the most iconic players in NBA history, Kobe Bryant won five NBA championships with the Lakers. He was known for his scoring ability, work ethic, and clutch performances, including his 81-point game in 2006.
